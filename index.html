    Tutorial

    Move with WASD, arrow keys or mobile arrows. White dots give you 1 point when eaten, and triangles give double. If youre larger than another player, you can eat them. Darker tiles, known as walls, block movement. Axes also appear on tiles, touching them gives 3 wall breaking uses [can stack.], breaking a wall gets rid of one use. Pressing the R key respawns you in a different area. Have fun!

    Tip: Camera follows you only when you move out of view.
